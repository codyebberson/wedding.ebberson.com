FATHER SEAN O'LAOIRE (Officiant)

Seán ÓLaoire is a Catholic priest and has known the Khilnani family for over 15 years. Born in Ireland, he was awarded a B.Sc. degree (major in Mathematics) from the National University of Ireland. He spent 14 years in East Africa working in Education, Agriculture, Preventative Medicine, Famine Relief, and Architecture and also with physically disabled children. He is multi-lingual and has an M.A. and a Ph.D. in transpersonal psychology. He is a co-founder and the Spiritual Director of a group of Bay Area Catholics, called "Companions on the Journey," who are attempting to find "a new way of being truly catholic." He is, also, a Clinical Psychologist,lectures, and conducts scientific research on the effects of prayer. His findings have been published in several research journals. In 1984, while living in Kenya, he wrote a book entitled Ukweli Ni Nini? (What does Truth Mean?) In 2003 he wrote, "Spirits in Spacesuits" and in 2006 he wrote, "Souls on Safari." In 2007 this was translated and published in German as "Seelen Auf Safari."
